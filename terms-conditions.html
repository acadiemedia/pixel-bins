Parents Info & Safety

🛡️ Safety Rules

- Stay off the road! All cleaning must be performed on the sidewalk or driveway. Never step into the street while working.

- Public View: Work must only be performed in open, public areas. Never enter a customer's home, garage, or backyard.

- Parental Supervision: We recommend parents or guardians accompany minors during all cleanings.

- Protective Gear: Always wear gloves and appropriate clothing.

📋 Directory Information

- Privacy: We use neighborhood names (like Cowan Heights) instead of exact streets to protect the privacy of our young cleaners.

- Fundraising: Use the "Group/Team" field to let neighbors know if you are raising money for a specific cause like Scouts, Soccer, or a school trip.

- Payments: All payment arrangements are handled directly between the customer and the cleaner's parents.

⚠️ Disclaimer

Pixel Bin Cleaners is a community directory. We do not vet users or facilitate payments. Use at your own risk and prioritize safety at all times.
